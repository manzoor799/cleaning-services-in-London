Our Expertise

Asphalt paving, block paving, driveway resurfacing, road marking, and civil works.

Asphalt resurfacing is the process of applying a new layer of asphalt over an existing paved surface. It is a cost-effective way to repair and extend the life of your driveway or parking lot.

The lifespan of asphalt paving depends on various factors, including climate, traffic, and maintenance. With proper care, asphalt paving can last 15-20 years or more.

Block paving, also known as brick paving, is a popular choice for driveways, patios, and walkways. It involves laying individual blocks or bricks in various patterns to create a durable and aesthetically pleasing surface.
